# Essay Series Roadmap V2

## Working rule

- Category first
- Company optional
- No forced Cars24 hook
- Each essay should work even if every company mention is removed

## 1. The Coming Decade of Car Ownership in India

India will not become a mass car-owning society by replaying the path of rich countries; it will do it through a trust-led, pre-owned mobility system built at scale.

## 2. The Greenest Car in India Is the One Already Built

The biggest near-term climate lever in Indian mobility is not only cleaner new production, but extending the useful life of the vehicles whose embodied carbon has already been paid.

## 3. India's Road Deaths Are a Trust Problem, Not a Traffic Problem

Road deaths persist not only because drivers make mistakes, but because the system around those mistakes is too opaque, fragmented, and unreliable to stop error from becoming fatal.

## 4. AI-Native Is Not AI-First

Most companies are adding AI to old workflows; the real divide of the next decade is between companies that use AI and companies rebuilt around it.

## 5. Builder Is the Only Role Left

In an AI-compressed, high-agency company, the only durable role is the person who can close loops directly rather than manage distance from the work.

## 6. The Car Is the Artifact. Trust Is the Product.

The enduring business in mobility is not selling a vehicle once, but making a household's relationship to that vehicle honest across financing, safety, resale, and use.
