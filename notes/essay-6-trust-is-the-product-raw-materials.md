# Essay 6 Raw Materials

## Title

- `The Car Is the Artifact. Trust Is the Product.`

## Core thesis

The enduring business in mobility is not simply moving a vehicle once. It is creating certainty around that vehicle across information, paperwork, financing, transfer, safety, and recourse.

## Strongest arguments

- used-car trust is built across multiple layers, not from inspection alone
- transfer certainty is part of the product experience, not an afterthought
- legitimacy with the state changes what a marketplace can credibly offer
- public policy is not outside the product when the category depends on state systems for truth and transfer
