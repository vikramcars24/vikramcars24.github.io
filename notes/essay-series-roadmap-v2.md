# Essay Series Roadmap V2

## Working rule

- Category first
- Company optional
- No forced Cars24 hook
- Each essay should work even if every company mention is removed

## 1. The Coming Decade of Car Ownership in India

India's next ownership wave will not look like the rich-world past; it will be pre-owned, transfer-heavy, and trust-led.

## 2. The Greenest Car in India Is the One Already Built

The biggest near-term climate lever in Indian mobility is not only cleaner new production, but making existing vehicles trustworthy enough to stay useful longer.

## 3. India's Road Deaths Are a Trust Problem, Not a Traffic Problem

Road deaths persist not only because drivers make mistakes, but because the system around those mistakes is too opaque, fragmented, and unreliable to stop error from becoming fatal.

## 4. AI-Native Is Not AI-First

Most companies are adding AI to old workflows; the real divide is between companies that buy AI tools and companies that rebuild around faster truth and agency.

## 5. Builder Is the Only Role Left

In an AI-compressed, org-first company, the only durable role is the person who can close loops directly rather than manage distance from the work.

## 6. The Car Is the Artifact. Trust Is the Product.

The enduring business in mobility is not selling a vehicle once, but becoming the trust layer around ownership across financing, transfer, safety, resale, and use.
